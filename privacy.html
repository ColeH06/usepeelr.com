Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Peelr (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, and protects information when you use our AI shopping assistant. Peelr is operated by Peel AI LLC in New Jersey, United States.

2. Information We Collect

A. Information You Provide

  • Email address (for login through Firebase Authentication)
  • Name or profile data from Google/Microsoft sign-in
  • Preferences and chat history (to improve recommendations)

B. Automatically Collected Information

  • IP address (via Cloudflare logs)
  • Device type, browser type, general location
  • Usage analytics and search activity
  • Cookies for ads, analytics, and personalization

3. How We Use Information

  • To deliver accurate product recommendations
  • To personalize shopping results
  • To improve Peelr’s performance and reliability
  • To prevent fraud or abuse

4. Third-Party Services

Peelr integrates with retailers, analytics providers, Shopnomix (for sponsored ads), Firebase Authentication, and Cloudflare. These services may receive certain technical information as part of your usage.

5. Your Choices

  • You may request deletion of your account
  • You may request deletion of your chat history
  • You may choose not to sign in (guest mode)

6. Contact Us

If you have questions, email us at contact@usepeelr.com.
