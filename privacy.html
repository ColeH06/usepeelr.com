Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Peelr (“we”, “our”, “us”) collects and uses information when you visit usepeelr.com or use our AI shopping assistant. This is a simplified placeholder version intended for product review and will be updated as new features are added.

1. Information We Collect

Peelr currently collects only the minimum amount of information necessary for the website to function:

  • Technical data (IP address, device type, browser type, general location). This is automatically collected by Cloudflare for security and performance.
  • Search queries entered into Peelr. These are used only to provide AI-generated responses and product results.

Peelr does not collect:

  • Personal account information
  • Login credentials
  • Sensitive personal data

If optional account features (Google login, saved favorites, etc.) are added later, this policy will be updated before launch.

2. How We Use Information

  • To return search results and product recommendations
  • To improve performance, stability, and accuracy
  • To protect the site from abuse and maintain security

3. Cookies and Analytics

Peelr may use basic cookies or analytics tools for:

  • Site performance
  • Security and traffic protection (Cloudflare)
  • Anonymous usage analytics

Peelr does not use cookies for advertising or cross-site tracking.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent personal information. Limited technical data may be processed by:

  • Cloudflare (security, routing, performance)
  • OpenAI (processing search text to generate results)

These providers only receive the information necessary to operate the service.

5. Data Retention

Search queries may be temporarily stored to improve service functionality. No personally identifiable information is stored.

6. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated as Peelr evolves. The latest version will always be available on this page.
